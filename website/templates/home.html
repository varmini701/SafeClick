About

CatchPhish is a dedicated platform committed to protecting individuals and organizations from the pervasive threat of phishing attacks. Phishing attacks, a form of cybercrime, attempt to deceive users into disclosing sensitive information such as passwords, financial details, and personal data by masquerading as trustworthy entities.

Remedies and Recommendations

If you've fallen victim to a phishing website, act fast! Change your passwords, enable two-factor authentication, and notify the relevant institution. Monitor your account activity, run a virus scan, and update your software and browsers. Be cautious of follow-up attacks and consider using a password manager. Finally, keep an eye on your credit reports. By taking these steps, you can minimize damage and secure your online presence.
